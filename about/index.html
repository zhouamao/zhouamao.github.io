ZHOU XIN (周欣)

Zhou Xin received Bachelor degree in remote sensing science and technology, in 2018 from China University of Geosciences (CUG), Wuhan, where he worked on synthetic aperture radar (SAR) image processing. He is currently working toward the Master degree in photogrammetry and remote sensing in CUG with focus on monitoring of soil freeze-thaw cycles.
